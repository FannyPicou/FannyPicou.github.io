Welcome to my website

I am a post-doctoral researcher at Université Côte d'Azur, in the Geoazur Laboratory. My research focuses on the numerical modeling of hydrological processes (i) on the watershed scale, for decision support in water resources management, and (ii) on the global scale, to advance the hydrology of climate models.
